## The Format

Each episode follows "The Pivot" narrative structure:

1. **The Cold Open** - The dramatic contrast between failure and success
2. **The Context** - What was the standard of care when this drug was created?
3. **The Origin** - Why was it originally designed? Who created it?
4. **The Struggle** - Why did it fail at its first indication?
5. **The Pivot Point** - How was the side effect discovered?
6. **The Renaissance** - How did the company pivot? What regulatory battles ensued?
7. **The Impact** - Financial results, patient impact, cultural significance
8. **The Playbook** - Key themes and lessons about drug repurposing success
