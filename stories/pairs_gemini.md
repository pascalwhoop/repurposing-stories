**Note on "Status":**

-   **Approved:** The regulatory body (FDA/EMA) has officially granted marketing authorization for
    the new indication.
-   **Standard of Care:** The drug is considered a primary treatment option in clinical guidelines,
    regardless of original label.
-   **Off-label:** Widely used by physicians for the new purpose based on evidence, even if not
    formally FDA-approved for that specific label (e.g., Metformin for PCOS in some regions).
